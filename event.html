Join MS Action Days!

ActionAid Denmark is for the first-time organizing Action Days, which will take place on the 18th and 19th of September. Mark your calendar now and join us for a weekend full of activism and inspiration!

The two days will include exciting workshops and talks about climate justice, anti-discrimination, activism and bring new perspectives and inspiration. The weekend will also be the perfect opportunity to meet and connect with new people who all wants to join the fight for a more equal and sustainable world.

The program will start on Saturday and end Sunday afternoon, and include meals and the possibility of staying at our Globalhagen hostel for the night. The event is open for everyone to join – members of ActionAid Denmark, volunteers and activists and people who wants to be a part of the fight for a better world.

The program of the weekend will both have workshops and talks in Danish and English. More information about the specific program will come soon.

It is free to participate, and to sign-up for the Action Days, all you have to do is follow this link: https://www.ms.dk/actiondays
